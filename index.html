Common Questions

Are these documents legally valid in the UAE?
Qanoni AI generates professionally drafted templates based on UAE law. We recommend having a licensed UAE lawyer review any document before execution.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No lock-in contracts. Cancel from your dashboard at any time. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
Do you support Free Zone companies?
Yes — DIFC, ADGM, DMCC, JAFZA and all major UAE free zones are covered in our compliance modules and document templates.
Is my data secure?
All documents are processed with end-to-end encryption. We do not store document content without your consent. Business and Enterprise plans include data residency options.
